Welcome to Recipe Suggestor Tool!

To request a recipe suggestion, follow the following steps:

1. Under the title, enter in up to *two* ingredients you have on hand one at a time:
	- Type in your ingredient (singular)
	- Click 'Add'
	- Entered ingredient will display to the right of the input box.

2. Click 'Search', a recipe will be suggested to you.

3. Click 'See Directions for This Recipe' to see full instructions for the recipe suggested.

4. If you want to see a different recipe using the same ingredients, click 'See Another Recipe'

To reset the search, click 'Reset' under the ingredient input box.
